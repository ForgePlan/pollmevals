# pollmevals-garden: Spring Planting Companion

Spring is the perfect time to start your garden and this is a guide for
that. We talk soil, seeds, water. No CLI here.

## Soil prep

Clear plot of weed and debris. Test soil pH with home test kit cheap
one. Most vegetable like slightly acidic, about 6.0–6.8. Add compost
or manure two week before.

## Seeds

Pick seed for your hardiness zone. Cool crop — lettuce, pea, radish —
go in as soon as soil workable. Warm crop tomato pepper bean wait
after last frost.

## Water

Most vegetable need one inch water per week. Water deep but not often,
encourage deep root. Mulch heavy to keep moisture and stop weed.
